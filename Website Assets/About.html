STEVEN DEPREZ

This site shows a selection of projects and artworks of Ostend based graphic designer and illustrator Steven Deprez.

He is working independently and collaboratively. After studying in Ghent and Los Angeles he graduated at LUCA School of Arts with the Master of Arts in Graphic Design.

With a strong focus on illustration, 3D, typography, animation, and a conceptual approach, he designs and develops visual identities and a wide array of printed and digital materials such as artworks, publications, books, visuals and posters for clients in the fields of art, culture and commercial fields.

Beside of commissioned work in the field of graphic design, he works as a visual artist and illustrator.
